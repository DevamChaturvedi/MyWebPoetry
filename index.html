What do we do?
In the nightmare of the dark All the dogs of Europe bark, And the living nations wait, Each sequestered in its hate…

Why us?
And the ship with eight sails And fifty cannons Will sail off with me.

Thoughts to live by

Stay a little longer! The storm shall subside as the time pass.
